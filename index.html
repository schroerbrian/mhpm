Orange Village is a 55+ mobile home park situated behind a beautiful and quiet residential neighborhood in Orange County, just minutes from the 55, 57, and 91 freeways.  We're a short walk to restaurants, grocery stores, and other shopping.  At least one permanent resident of each mobile home must be 55 years of age or older and all other residents must be at least 18.  Orange Village Mobile Home Park is one of the best leased-land communities in Orange County, California.  The low monthly lot lease fee covers exclusive tenancy of your own home's private yard and carport as well as use of the community recreational and laundry facilities.  Own your own mobile home with attached private parking without the responsibilities of caring for a large yard or the recreational facilities.
